# PowerClaw Marketing Copy

## Developer Tooling

PowerClaw is a self-hosted AI operator for your codebase.

It inspects your repo, plans changes, requests approval before risky commands,
runs approved checks, remembers repeatable workflows, and prepares deployments
from one native Python runtime.

No black-box agent maze. No fragile wrapper chain. Just one runtime you control.

## Self-Hosted AI

Your agent. Your server. Your rules.

Run PowerClaw on a private EC2 instance with SQLite memory, systemd, backups,
auth, and a local dashboard. Keep your agent close to your infrastructure while
giving it the tools to help operate your projects.

Powerful enough to work. Restrained enough to trust.

## Hybrid Agent Platform

The best ideas from OpenClaw and Hermes, rebuilt as one clean runtime.

PowerClaw combines gateway-ready architecture, learned skills, safe tool
execution, memory, approvals, and deployment workflows into a Python-native
agent system.

Not a wrapper. Not a meta-agent. A new operating layer.

## Engineering Ops

Turn repo work into repeatable operations.

PowerClaw can inspect your workspace, reason through deployment risk, request
terminal approvals, summarize what happened, and save successful procedures as
reusable skills.

It does not just answer questions. It helps build your runbook.

## Trust And Safety

AI automation with a brake pedal.

PowerClaw denies privileged commands by default, records approval requests, logs
runtime events, and keeps every session traceable. It is designed for operators
who want AI assistance without handing over the keys.

Useful automation starts with control.

## Telegram And Remote Agent Future

Talk to your engineering agent from anywhere.

PowerClaw is being built for chat and device gateways, starting with
Telegram-style workspace access. The same runtime that serves the CLI and
dashboard will power remote conversations, approvals, and workflows.

One brain. Many surfaces.
